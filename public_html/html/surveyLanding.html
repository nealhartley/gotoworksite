Team Path Starters are working through the R9 Accelerator as part of a better of business initiative. Their mission is to provide fulfilling employment for those who are stuck on their journey. 57,000 people are currently receiving health related benefit due to the impact their mental health condition has on their ability to work. Mental health conditions have accounted for 97% of the growth in health-related benefits in the last 10 years, and this is part of an international trend.People diagnosed with mental health conditions often have poor employment outcomes. There are a number of barriers that are preventing people from finding and keeping a job. We want to know what these barriers are and we want to discover a way to break through them. This is where you come in, please take our survey and offer us insight into how mental health affects all of New Zealand
